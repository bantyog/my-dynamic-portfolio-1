Questions

Frequently asked questions

How are your caregivers vetted?
Every candidate undergoes identity verification, full background and reference checks, professional skills assessment and an in-person interview before they are eligible for placement.
Can I hire short-term staff?
Yes. We offer hourly, daily, weekly, monthly and long-term placements — including live-in and live-out arrangements to fit your schedule.
Do you provide UK placements?
Yes — our UK office handles placements across the United Kingdom, subject to eligibility, visa status and role requirements.
How quickly can services begin?
For most domestic and care roles, placements begin within 3–7 days of the initial consultation. Specialist or executive roles may take a little longer.
What if the placement isn't the right fit?
We offer a replacement guarantee within the first 30 days. If something isn't working, we'll match you with a new professional at no extra cost.
